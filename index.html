FAB IQ

Preserving semiconductor manufacturing intelligence before it walks out the door.

When senior fab engineers leave, their troubleshooting instinct usually leaves with them — most manufacturing systems track machines and metrics, not the reasoning behind a fix. FAB IQ converts troubleshooting reports, maintenance logs, and incident records into a searchable knowledge graph, so the next time a similar issue surfaces, it surfaces the past case, the likely root cause, and what actually worked — aimed at India's growing semiconductor ecosystem.

AI / Knowledge GraphsSemiconductor MfgEnterprise SaaS
Prototype completed — Certified, AMD Lemonade Developer Challenge

VoltSpectra

Catching an electronic fault while it's still a whisper, not a failure.

Most electronic failures don't happen instantly — they begin as small drifts in voltage, current, or signal behaviour that go unnoticed until something actually breaks. VoltSpectra is an exploratory fault-analysis tool built to flag those early deviations before they become downtime, aimed at industrial automation, power electronics, and IoT systems.

Lemonade LLMGemmaPythonJSONPredictive Maintenance
Team build — Google Solution Challenge (not yet deployed)

CommunityPulse

Closing the gap between a community's need and a volunteer actually showing up.

NGOs in Tamil Nadu often run on paper surveys and phone chains — urgent needs get missed, and rural citizens without smartphones go unheard entirely. CommunityPulse digitises field reports, scores urgency with AI, and auto-matches volunteers to tasks, with Seva Maiyam kiosk and Tamil IVR support so reporting never requires literacy or a smartphone.
